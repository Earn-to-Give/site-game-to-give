How it Works

FIND A GAME

Select from your favorite sports to find an upcoming game. Choose a max reward amount for each game (e.g. $10) and select the teams you think will win.

SUBMIT PICKS

The bigger the underdog, the cheaper the pick! If your team only has a 30% chance of winning, you'll only pay $3.00. In this example, your opponent would pay $7.00.

WIN & GIVE

If you win the game, your Game to Give™ Balance will receive the max reward (e.g. $10). You can play more games or choose to donate a portion or all of your balance to any of the $1.7M charities in the USA.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are your chocolates so special?

Mary’s Brigadeiro are made with the absolute finest chocolates source from around the world with a high percent of cocoa fresh that means we just use pure, real and the healthy chocolate combined with local ingredients. Each Brigadeiro has its own flavour profile and much care is placed in pairing the Brigadeiro recipe with the perfect percent of cocoa.

Do the brigadeiros need to be refrigerated?

Real food doesn’t last too long and our Brigadeiros is a delicate sweet, so Mary recommends to you EAT FRESH and consume them at room’s temperature.

  • Keep them refrigerated to maintain your Brigadeiros with a soft and smooth texture
  • Bring back to the room temperature around 18 ºC / 21ºC;
  • Eat fresh! Best if consumed within 12 days from production date.

    When will my order be shipped or delivered?

    All our chocolates are freshly finely crafted daily in Toronto. We use a courier service to ensure all orders arrive safely across all Canada. We need at least 2 business day to produce your order.

    You will receive your special order in around 1-3 business day, so make sure to order between Monday - Wednesday to receive them until Friday if you live outside Toronto and GTA area.

    For any urgent deliveries (same day or the next day), please contact us asap or send an email to contact@marysbsweets.com and we will try to accommodate them.

    Our shipping system monitors the temperatures where your package is being delivered to ensure your gift arrives in perfect condition, no matter the weather. We tuck our elegant gift boxes inside crisp to create the perfect gifting experience. Upon your request, we’ll also include a complimentary Note Card message

    Can I pick up my order at your facility?

    Of course! We produce our Brigadeiros in a Food Business Incubator Food Starter located at 76 Densley Ave, Toronto at different time and dates during the week. If you wish to pick your order at this location, you must to schedule and see the availability for the requested date and time or we will be happy to accommodate a suitable delivery or shipment methods and you will receive them directly to your door.

They are always serving fresh (made pre order) that results a creamier and smoother texture than the other kind of chocolate confectionery.
